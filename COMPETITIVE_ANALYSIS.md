# Rekrut AI — Competitive Analysis: AI Capabilities Across 16 Modules

**Date:** February 9, 2026
**Purpose:** Map the AI capabilities landscape across every Rekrut AI module, identify where competitors are genuinely AI-native vs. marketing fluff, and define where Rekrut AI can leapfrog.

---

## Executive Summary

1. **No competitor offers a unified, AI-native hiring platform across all 16 modules.** Every player is strong in 2-3 areas and weak everywhere else. Rekrut AI's opportunity is being the first truly end-to-end AI-native platform.
2. **OmniScore (two-sided scoring) has zero direct competitors.** Glassdoor does employer ratings. ATS platforms do candidate scoring. Nobody combines both into a single scoring system inside the hiring workflow.
3. **Most "AI-powered" claims are marketing labels.** Real AI (ML-trained models, generative AI, agentic workflows) exists at HireVue, Workday, Deel, and LinkedIn. Everyone else is keyword matching with a chatbot bolted on.
4. **The compliance moat is real.** NYC LL144, EU AI Act (high-risk deadline August 2026), and California's ADS regulations create massive barriers. Platforms that bake in compliance win enterprise deals.
5. **Global payroll is the most AI-advanced category.** Deel ($17.3B valuation) and Papaya Global are leading with agentic AI agents. Rekrut AI needs strong partnerships or deep investment here.

---

## Strategic Priorities

1. **OmniScore** — No competitor has two-sided scoring. Build first, market loudly.
2. **AI-Native Assessment + Matching** — Own the full pipeline from skills test to job match with real ML.
3. **Compliance as a Feature** — EU AI Act deadline Aug 2026. Built-in bias auditing = enterprise sales.
4. **Global Payroll via Partnership** — Integrate Deel/Remote/Papaya APIs, don't build from scratch.
5. **AI Interview Coaching (Both Sides)** — Coach candidates AND evaluate interviewers in one system.

## Key Finding: AI-Native vs AI-Bolted-On

Every major competitor added AI as an afterthought. HireVue (founded 2004, AI ~2016), Greenhouse (2012, AI 2024), Workday (2005, acquired HiredScore 2024). Only Deel (2019) approaches AI-native in the payroll space. Rekrut AI, building in 2026, has the unique opportunity to design every module with AI at the core from day one.
